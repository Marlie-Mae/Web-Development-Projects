Ivory and Ebony

July 6, 2023 (ns)

- by Ms.SweetPotato

			Ivory and Ebony
                        Two different symphony
                        But when they collides, creates a beautiful
                        melody-
                        That makes the world in harmony
                        
                        Indeed, opposites attracts
                        Both works and constract-
                        Of this beautiful abstract

                        They may be the exact opposite of each other 
                        But when they are together
                        They are better than your lover
                        Because they can be together, forever
