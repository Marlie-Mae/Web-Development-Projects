To Hold

July 6, 2023 (ns)

- by Ms.SweetPotato

			to hold one's hand, it means you won't let go
                        despite of any circumtances you will still
                        hold on and never let go
                        to hold one's heart, it means you won't break it
                        loving it and cherishing it
                        being careful so it won't broke into pieces
                        to hold someone, it means you have to take
                        the responsibility
                        you close it, do your duty
                        do not forget to cherish her
                        do not forget you love her
                        never let go, do not break her
                        hold her tight, do your duty.
