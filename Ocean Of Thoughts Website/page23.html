Lost

July 6, 2023 (ns)

- by Ms.SweetPotato

			in this time i just want to cry
                        standing nowhere, i should make decision for 
                        my life
                        lost in my track, i continue to walk with a 
                        heavy sigh
                        having no reason, with a strong grip in the knife
                        slowly walking in this long road
                        confused in which direction i should go 
                        should i go to the left to lessen my heavy load?
                        or to the right, which leads, i don't know?
                        is it worth this life?
                        is it worth the fight?
                        is it okay to give up?
                        i hope it's alright
                        i'm tired in this long journey
                        i want to close my eyes
                        go to the place where happiness lies
