Untitled

July 6, 2023 (ns)

- by Ms.SweetPotato

			Your voice is my guiding light.
                        A sparrow cries out.
                        Looking for me within the nameless sky.
                        I will lead for tomorrow in this cradle woven
                        from kindness.
                        Be it a day of rain or a day of sunshine.
                        I will be there to protect you.
                        A treasure more valuable from them all.
                        A nameless flower,
                        Lies in peace.
