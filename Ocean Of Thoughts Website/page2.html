Wrath

March 16, 2022 (ns)

- by Ms.SweetPotato

			I want to rip their faces, 
                        Smash their heads,
                        And throw their bodies in the burning flames 
                        of despair.
                        I want to hear their sweet screams;
                        I love to watch their sufferings,
                        Until it satifies me to the fullest.
                        Just by imagining it, makes me feel 
                        I'm the greatest.
